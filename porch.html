Open Porch

Weekly listening sessions — come by, say hello, and share what you’re seeing on the ground.

What to expect

Meet, listen, and solve locally

Pull up a chair and tell me what’s working, what isn’t, and what you’d fix first. If you need accessibility accommodations, note them below and I’ll make it work.

Every week Wednesdays, 4–6 pm 81 May St, Biddeford
  • Stop in any time during the listed hour.
  • Neighbors of all viewpoints welcome.
  • Not a town hall — it’s a listening porch.
